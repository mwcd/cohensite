ABOUT.

Beth Cohen tells big, meaningful stories for clients doing their part to make the world a better place. She believes in honesty, hard work, creativity and a little magic.

For the past six years, she has served as Director of Media at PopTech, developing content strategy, setting up content teams to run like clockwork, and producing multimedia stories about initiatives across the globe. She considers it an honor to have partnered with organizations ranging from small NGOs to large corporations.

Today she works with clients on a consultancy basis. She especially loves producing videos on the ground in challenging locations.
