PROJECT DETAILS

BACKGROUND

When violence threatens to break out on city streets, in the run-up to an election, or in a land dispute, how can community leaders instantly reach out to urge calm and quash false rumors? PopTech teamed up with Sisi Ni Amani in Kenya to drive subscriptions to their novel mobile violence prevention service and craft behavior-change SMS messaging models.

BRIEF

PopTech wanted to document the work of its initiative PeaceTXT in order to connect with hearts and minds, both in one-on-one meetings and in larger presentations, such as the PopTech stage. Stories are integral to how PopTech extends the reach of its initiatives and part of how it scales its work.

CONCEPT

We created a concept built around an original interview with one of the youth involved in the project, and filmed a couple days in his life while we were already in Nairobi to document a PopTech event.

RESPONSE

This short and other videos from this series were featured on PBS Need to Know online and Forbes and the partner organizations use this video over and over.
